What is DTube?

DTube is a video platform, that rewards it's users for creating and curating videos. DTube runs on a decentralized public blockchain, which means it is run by a free and open community around it, without any central authority involved. Therefore censorship on DTube is close to impossible. It is free to use for anyone and it even pays you for doing so.

The asset of modern day's society is your attention and advertisers pay huge amounts of money to get it. In the "old model" user generated content is used to gain attention and then selling a part of it to advertisers. Unfortunately the price the advertiser pays for just a little bit of your attention is much more than what the creator who's content attracted your attention in the first place, is actually rewarded with. That's because a middle man is needed in order to connect advertisers, creators and consumers.

DTube on the other hand doesn't need a middle man and is free of payed advertisements. And yet it transforms the value users generate by creating quality content and contributing to the community, into a practical and tradable asset and currency that has real world value.
